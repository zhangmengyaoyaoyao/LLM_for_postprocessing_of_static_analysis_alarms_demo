In this case, the use of `System.gc()` is explicit and not necessary. The garbage collector is designed to run automatically when the JVM needs to free up memory, and explicit calls to it can actually hurt performance.

Therefore, this warning is actionable, as the developer can remove the explicit call to `System.gc()` to improve performance.
